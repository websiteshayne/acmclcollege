Our Courses

Bachelor in Secondary Education

Major in Math

The Mathematics major focuses on equipping BSEd students with a strong foundation in mathematical concepts and teaching methodologies. Students engage in advanced coursework covering topics such as algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics. They learn to apply problem-solving strategies and develop logical reasoning skills.

Bachelor in Secondary Education

Major in English

Students pursuing the English major within the BSEd course immerse themselves in the study of language, literature, and communication. They acquire in-depth knowledge of grammar, phonetics, and linguistics, enabling them to teach English effectively.
